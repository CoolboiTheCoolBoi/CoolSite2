PROBLEM
Why were Men considered to be the primary wage earner while Women were expected to be the one who stays inside the house and takes care of the children?
CHOSEN SOLUTION
To establish an organization which aims to reduce and eliminate gender stereotypes. This 
 organization will encourage people to participate in eliminating gender stereotypes by our poster 
 campaign.  This way, people would not get worried about being accepted into a workplace just 
 because they have different gender than the common one found in that workplace.  This is
  because both gender should be treated fairly:  in political, in social, in economic & cultural terms.

 We encourage every individual of any ages to participate in our poster’s campaign about the 
 Gender Inequality that is happening here in our society.  The purpose why we’re doing this is to 
 help other people whose struggling with the issue and to promote and encourage a general 
 understanding of gender-related challenges, for instance, violence against women and gender pay gap.

 This is what we hope to achieve from our prototype, the elimination of gender stereotypes will 
 help the chances of women and men being accepted in places, either work or politics, in school or 
 at home, even in public places or riding a public transportation.
